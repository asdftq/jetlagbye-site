The Science of
Circadian Rhythm

Jet lag isn't just tiredness‚Äîit's a disconnect between your internal body clock and the external world. Your circadian rhythm controls sleep, hormones, metabolism, and mood.

Light is the key. Properly timed light exposure is the only proven way to shift your circadian clock quickly.

Timing matters more than duration. Light at the wrong time can make jet lag worse, not better.

East vs. West matters. Traveling east is harder because advancing your clock is tougher than delaying it.

Recovery takes ~1 day per timezone. With proper intervention, we can cut that time significantly.
